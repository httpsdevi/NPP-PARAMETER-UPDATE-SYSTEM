Welcome to the NPPUS Help Desk

Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and guidance on using the Nuclear Power Plant Update System.

How do I access the NPPUS?

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Select 'NPPUS' from the menu.
  3. Enter your credentials if prompted.

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

  • Check your username and password for typos.
  • Ensure your account is in good standing.
  • Contact support if the issue persists.
